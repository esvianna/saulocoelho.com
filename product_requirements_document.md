Okay, I can definitely create a project PRD (Product Requirements Document) or a brief for you. However, I need **the context** first!

Please provide me with the details of your project. The more information you give me, the more comprehensive and accurate the PRD/brief will be.

Here's a template of the kind of information I'm looking for. Just fill in the blanks or tell me the story of your project, and I'll structure it into a formal document.

---

**Please Provide the Following Context:**

1.  **Project Name:** (What is the working title of this project?)

2.  **Executive Summary / High-Level Idea:** (In 1-2 sentences, what is this project about? What problem does it solve or opportunity does it seize?)

3.  **Problem Statement:**
    *   **What is the core problem we are trying to solve?** (Describe the pain point, inefficiency, or unmet need.)
    *   **Who experiences this problem?** (Our users, internal teams, customers, etc.)
    *   **What are the current solutions (if any) and why are they inadequate?**

4.  **Goals & Objectives:**
    *   **What do we aim to achieve with this project?** (Be specific and measurable if possible. E.g., "Increase user engagement by X%", "Reduce operational costs by Y%", "Enter new market segment Z".)
    *   **How will we measure success?** (Key Metrics / KPIs)

5.  **Target Audience:**
    *   **Who is this product/feature primarily for?** (E.g., "Existing premium subscribers," "New small business owners," "Our internal sales team.")
    *   **What are their key characteristics/needs related to this project?**

6.  **Scope - What's In & What's Out:**
    *   **What are the core features or functionalities that MUST be included in the initial release (MVP)?** (List them out simply.)
    *   **What is explicitly NOT included in this initial release, but might be considered for future phases?** (This is crucial for managing expectations.)

7.  **Key Features / Requirements (Describe what the user will experience):**
    *   **If you have specific user stories, list them:** (e.g., "As a [type of user], I want to [action], so that [benefit].")
    *   **Otherwise, describe the key functionalities:** (e.g., "Users can upload documents," "System will automatically generate reports," "Admin dashboard to manage users.")

8.  **Dependencies / Constraints:**
    *   **Are there any other projects, teams, or external systems this project relies on?**
    *   **Are there any technical, budget, resource, or timeline constraints we need to be aware of?**

9.  **Assumptions / Open Questions:**
    *   **What are we assuming to be true for this project to be successful?**
    *   **What are the major unanswered questions that need to be addressed?**

10. **Potential Risks:**
    *   **What could go wrong? What are the biggest challenges or unknowns?**

11. **Stakeholders:**
    *   **Who are the key people or departments involved or impacted by this project?** (e.g., Engineering, Design, Marketing, Sales, Legal, Operations, specific executives.)

12. **High-Level Timeline (Optional, if known):**
    *   **Are there any target dates or deadlines for launch?**

---

Once you provide this information, I will synthesize it into a structured PRD or a concise project brief, depending on the level of detail you provide and your preference.
